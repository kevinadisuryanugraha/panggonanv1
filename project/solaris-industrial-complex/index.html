Solaris Industrial Complex

Project Overview

Greenwood Office Park is a state-of-the-art commercial complex designed to blend modern aesthetics with functional, sustainable spaces. Located in a prime urban area, this project offers businesses a collaborative and energy-efficient environment.

Project Goals

The primary objective was to create a commercial space that encourages productivity and collaboration while integrating sustainable practices. Key goals included energy efficiency, open and versatile floor plans, and a harmonious connection with natural surroundings.

  • Reducing carbon footprint through sustainable materials and energy-efficient technology.

Design & Architecture

Greenwood Office Park features a contemporary aesthetic with an open layout, large glass facades, and eco-conscious landscaping. Its design emphasizes harmony with the environment through:

  1. Sustainable Materials: Use of reclaimed wood, recycled steel, and low-emission concrete.
  2. Natural Lighting: Strategic placement of floor-to-ceiling windows and skylights, minimizing the need for artificial lighting.
  3. Green Landscaping: Surrounding green roofs, native plantings, and water-efficient irrigation to foster a calming environment.
Key Features

Solar panels covering 30% of the roof area, along with energy-efficient HVAC systems and smart lighting. Flexible office layouts that support collaborative and private workspaces. Rooftop terraces, communal green spaces, and walking paths, providing a refreshing work environment.
